It is very odd that all characters showed up all on the same night to the Mr Bennett’s house right before he was found in a pool of blood….. lets dive deeper into why each member was there then shall we!

According to Mr Gold he was there peacefully to chat about the land Mr Bennett owns, you see Mr Gold wants that land for himself because he knows how valuable it is. How peaceful was this chat and why did the neighbours here shouting?

PennyFara openly admits she was there to snoop about for more dirt on Mr Bennett, she is adamant he is up to no good and the story would be fantastic! What did she find and why was Mr Bennett so angry?
